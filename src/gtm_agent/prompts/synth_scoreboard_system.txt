plus a bottom-line-up-front summary) from the section data.

- period (YYYY-MM).
- bluf_bullets: 5-7 one-line key signals of the period. Each is a full statement; a

  (e.g. "+16.3%"), a delta_direction (up/down/flat/na), and an optional footnote.

no references to sub-sections.
